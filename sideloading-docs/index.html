iOS Sideloading Docs

This is a guide on how to sideload apps on iOS devices using AltStore. Follow the steps below to get started:

Step 1: Download AltStore

Visit the official AltStore website at https://altstore.io and download the AltStore application for your computer (available for both Windows and macOS).

Step 2: Install AltStore on Your Computer

Follow the installation instructions provided on the AltStore website to install the application on your computer.

Step 3: Connect Your iOS Device

Connect your iOS device to your computer using a USB cable. Ensure that you trust the computer on your iOS device if prompted.

Step 4: Install AltStore on Your iOS Device

Open the AltStore application on your computer and follow the instructions to install AltStore on your iOS device.

Step 5: Trust AltStore on Your iOS Device

On your iOS device, go to Settings > General > Device Management (or Profiles & Device Management). Find the profile associated with AltStore and trust it.

Step 6: Download and Install Apps

With AltStore installed and trusted, you can now download and install apps. To do this:

  1. Open the AltStore app on your iOS device.
  2. Browse or search for the app you want to install.
  3. Follow the on-screen instructions to download and install the app.

Step 7: Refresh Apps Regularly

Apps installed via AltStore need to be refreshed every 7 days. To refresh an app:

  1. Open the AltStore app on your iOS device.
  2. Go to the "My Apps" tab.
  3. Tap the refresh icon next to the app you want to refresh.

Additional Tips

  • Ensure that your computer and iOS device are on the same Wi-Fi network for seamless installation and refreshing of apps.
  • If you encounter any issues, refer to the AltStore FAQ or support resources on their website.
  • Keep your AltStore application updated to the latest version for optimal performance and security.

By following these steps, you should be able to successfully sideload apps on your iOS device using AltStore. Enjoy exploring new apps!
